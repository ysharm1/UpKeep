## Potential Issues & Solutions

### Issue 1: "I'm only making $12 per booking, is that enough?"

**Answer: Yes, at scale.**
```
Month 1: 50 bookings √ó $12 = $600
Month 3: 200 bookings √ó $12 = $2,400
Month 6: 500 bookings √ó $12 = $6,000
Month 12: 1,000 bookings √ó $12 = $12,000
Year 2: 2,000 bookings √ó $12 = $24,000/month
```

**Plus:**
- Low costs (hosting ~$50/month)
- High margins (80%+ after Stripe)
- Scalable (more bookings = more revenue)

### Issue 2: "Providers might ghost after diagnostic"

**Solutions:**
- Require on-platform communication
- Track repair completion rates
- Ban providers who consistently ghost
- Incentivize on-platform repairs (reviews, ratings)
- Most providers won't ghost (diagnostic fee is good money)

### Issue 3: "What if diagnostic fee is too low?"

**You can adjust:**
- Start: $85 diagnostic fee
- Later: $95 or $100 if market supports it
- Your $12 fee stays the same
- Provider gets the increase

### Issue 4: "What about very expensive jobs?"

**It's fine:**
```
$5,000 HVAC replacement:
You still get: $12
Provider gets: $73 + $5,000 = $5,073
Provider cost: 0.2% (basically free!)
```

**Providers will LOVE you for big jobs.**
