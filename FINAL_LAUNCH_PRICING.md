# FINAL Launch Pricing - Diagnostic-First Model

## The Model (Simple & Clear)

### Standard Flow:
1. **Homeowner posts new problem**
2. **Diagnostic required** (not optional)
3. **Homeowner pays diagnostic fee:** $85 (provider sets it)
4. **You take:** 15% of diagnostic = $12.75
5. **Provider gets:** $72.25 for diagnostic visit
6. **Provider does diagnostic, gives repair quote**
7. **If repair approved:** Diagnostic fee credited toward repair
8. **Provider keeps:** 100% of repair work

### Your Revenue Per Job:
**$12.75** (15% of $85 diagnostic fee)

### Provider Cost:
**2.6%** of total revenue (diagnostic + repair)

---

## Why This Works

### ‚úÖ Adoption Side:
- Not taxing full repair
- Providers don't feel like you're skimming their real revenue
- $12.75 per job feels reasonable
- Much lower bypass incentive
- Mirrors real contractor behavior

### ‚úÖ Revenue Side:
- Every new job must pass through diagnostic
- You monetize every job entry
- Predictable income
- No subscription complexity yet
- Simple to explain and implement

### ‚úÖ Psychological Unlock:
**Diagnostic fee is credited toward repair**
- Homeowner feels: "I'm not losing money"
- Provider feels: "This is normal"
- You still take your 15% of diagnostic
- No friction
