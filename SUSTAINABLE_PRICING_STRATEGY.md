# Sustainable Pricing Strategy: The Real Economics

## You're Right: 15% + Stripe is Too Much

### The Brutal Math:
```
Provider: 40 jobs/month × $500 = $20,000 revenue
Your commission: 15% = $3,000
Stripe fees: ~3% = $600
Total platform cost: $3,600 (18% of revenue!)

Provider's gross margin: 15-30% = $3,000-6,000
After platform fees: $2,400-5,400 left
```

**You're taking 60-120% of their gross profit.** That's unsustainable.

---

## Why Traditional Marketplaces Struggle

### The Death Spiral:
1. Platform charges 15-20%
2. Providers try it, realize it's expensive
3. Providers ghost customers off-platform
4. Platform loses revenue
5. Platform raises fees to compensate
6. More providers leave
7. Death spiral

### This is why:
- Thumbtack has high churn
- Angi struggles with retention
- TaskRabbit can't keep providers

**They're all fighting the same problem you identified.**

## Alternative Business Models That Actually Work

### Model 1: Lead Generation (Not Commission) ⭐ RECOMMENDED

**How it works:**
- Homeowner posts job
- Providers pay to see job details and contact info
- No commission on the transaction
- Provider and homeowner transact directly

**Pricing:**
- Small jobs (<$500): $5-10 per lead
- Medium jobs ($500-2,000): $15-25 per lead
- Large jobs ($2,000+): $30-50 per lead

**Provider Economics:**
```
40 leads/month × $20 average = $800/month
Close rate: 50% = 20 jobs
Revenue: 20 × $500 = $10,000
Cost: $800 (8% of revenue)
Much more sustainable!
```

**Your Economics:**
```
100 providers × $800/month = $80,000/month
No Stripe fees on leads (just subscription)
Predictable revenue
```

**Pros:**
- ✅ Lower cost for providers (8% vs 18%)
- ✅ Predictable revenue for you
- ✅ No payment processing complexity
- ✅ Providers can't ghost (they already paid)
- ✅ Scales better

**Cons:**
- ❌ Providers pay upfront (risk for them)
- ❌ You don't benefit from large jobs
- ❌ Need to prevent lead fraud
- ❌ Harder to track job completion

**This is what Thumbtack and Angi actually do!** They call it "commission" but it's really pay-per-lead.

```
Much better than 18%!
```

**Your Economics:**
```

```

**Pros:**
- ✅ Predictable MRR

- ✅ Providers committed (paying monthly)
- ✅ Can offer 0% commission at high tier
- ✅ Sustainable for both sides

**Cons:**

- ❌ Still need payment processing

### Model 3: Diagnostic Fee Only (Your Original Idea) ⭐ MOST INNOVATIVE

- Provider keeps 100% of repair quote
- No commission on actual repair work

**Pricing:**
```
Diagnostic fee: $85

Repair work: $500
Provider keeps: $500 (100%!)
```

**Provider Economics:**
```
40 diagnostic visits × $72 = $2,880
40 repairs × $500 = $20,000
Total revenue: $22,880
Platform cost: $510 (2.2% of total revenue!)
```

**Your Economics:**
```
1,000 diagnostics/month × $12.75 = $12,750/month
Sustainable and fair!
```

**Pros:**
- ✅ Extremely low cost for providers (2-3%)
- ✅ Providers love it (keep repair revenue)
- ✅ Homeowners protected (paid diagnostic)
- ✅ Your unique differentiator
- ✅ Sustainable for everyone

**Cons:**
- ❌ Lower revenue per transaction for you
- ❌ Need high volume to make money
- ❌ Providers could ghost after diagnostic
- ❌ Hard to enforce repair happens on platform

**This is your competitive advantage!** Don't abandon it.
