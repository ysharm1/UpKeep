## Solution: Hybrid Monetization Model

### Revenue Stream 1: Diagnostic Fees (When Applicable)

**New problems requiring assessment:**
- Homeowner pays $85 diagnostic
- You take $15
- Provider gets $70
- Diagnostic credited toward repair if approved

### Revenue Stream 2: Booking Fee (All Jobs)

**For ALL jobs (diagnostic or direct quote):**
- Small booking/connection fee: $5-10
- Charged when provider accepts job
- Covers platform costs, payment processing, messaging
- Much smaller than diagnostic fee
- Feels like "service fee" not "toll booth"

### Revenue Stream 3: Subscriptions (Optional, Later)

**Pro providers ($99/month):**
- Unlimited bookings (no $5-10 fee per job)
- Featured placement
- Priority support
- Advanced tools
